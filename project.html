Canvas: Randomised Circles

Project-1

Making random colored circles of random sizes move in random directions at random places and bounce off the wall. Using the HTML5 Canvas tag, and alot of JavaScript. The circles within a certain range of the mouse increase in size and then back down to their original size. The canvas is adaptive to screen resize and reates more circles dynamically.
